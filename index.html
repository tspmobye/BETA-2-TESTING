CLUB ARCHIVE

Club Archive is a website specifically designed to aid students who struggle with navigating clubs found in Bacolod Tay Tung High School.

It aims to provide detailed information and help students locate their desired clubs with minimal to no trouble.

WHO ARE WE?

THE DEVELOPERS

We are a team of Grade 10 programming students learning how to create a website using HTML and CSS for our IPT.

This website is dedicated to students who struggle to find information about school clubs.

合氣道 (Aikido Club)

The Tay Tung Aikido Club is a martial arts club inspired by the philosophy of Harmony.

Aikido directly translates as The Way of Harmony.

Album

Rules

  1. Attend all scheduled training unless with a valid excuse
  2. Always observe proper hygiene
  3. Always clean and take care of Dojo (Training Hall), training equipement, and uniform
  4. Always respect the Sensei, classmates, and guests
  5. Extend training discipline beyond the Dojo
  6. Be responsible for personal development
  7. Help others develop themselves
  8. Always inform the Sensei of urgent and important matters

Programs

  • Keiko - basic training
  • Kyu grade exams
  • Martial Arts Seminars – ex. Visayas Aikido Fellowship
